I'm a PhD student at Cornell University. I'm interested in machine learning for code generation and symbolic reasoning tasks. Previously I worked at Google and X on large language models for code. Previously I worked in Preferred Neworks. Before that I was a research assistant in Academia Sinica. I worked on design and analysis of efficient algorithms to solve several important problems arising from cryptology, as well as the development and implementation of these algorithms on CPUs and GPUs.
